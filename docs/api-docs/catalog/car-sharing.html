Service Description
This service facilitates the FordPass car share service and allows for activities such as managing favorites, locations and reservations.

Service Capabilities

  • Retrieving, adding, and deleting a favorite
  • Retrieving, adding, updating, and deleting a reservation
  • Retrieving a group of reservations
  • Retrieving, and updating an account
  • Retrieving stations, vehicle data, and location or group of locations
  • Performing a search
  • Make a reservation
  • Logging a user out
